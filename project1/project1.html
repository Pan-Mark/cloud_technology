Tokyo Tower

Tokyo tower which was based off of the Eiffel Tower. This tower was the tallest structure in japan when it was built until 2010 when the Skytree was built. The tower is 333 meters tall. The top room is at 250 meters tall, giving a great view to anyone up there.

Tokyo Skytree

The Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in Tokyo Japan after taking its place from the Tokyo Tower in 2010. It was officially opened to the opened in May of 2012. The tower is a whole 634 meters tall and has 2 observation decks at 350 meters and 450 meters. Lookig from these decks at night gives a beautiful view of Tokyo.
