Why Choose Tokyo?

Tokyo is a truly unique place to visit. There is also a lot of tall concrete buildings in tokyo with some traditional temples and shrines sprinkled around. many of the buildings also have neon lights on them. There are a lot of sights to see in Tokyo. It also has a lot of tasty and unique foods. Tokyo is well known for their food since they have the most Michelin Stars than any other city.

reserve spots to famous places of Tokyo and experience all the unique sights and cruisines. There are a lot more things to do there, but you would have to see for yourself!

Activities to do

Resturants

Japan is well known for their large amount of cruisines. Tokyo being the city with the most Michelin Stars out of any city, and being the capital of Japan, has alot of resturants selling good quality and tasting food.

Vending Machine

Tokyo has alot of vending machines selling all kinds of stuff, from hot and cold drinks to pizza and ramen. These machines also sell non-food items too like umbrellas and shoes. These machines are a sight in of itself.

Towers

There are many tall towers in Tokyo, but you need a reservation to enter and take a view from above. It is worth it though since the amount of neon lights in Tokyo makes the view of Tokyo city at night a beautiful sight.

Old buildings

There are many shrines and traditional temples dotted around. These shrines and temples are a sight to behold and the guides for the temples gives a up close view of the history Japan and what was it like in those times.

Akihabara

Akihabara or also called by many "Electric City" is a place full of technology, old and new. Many fans flock there for the large amount of old and new games. The place is also famous for the amount of Manga and Anime related stuff sold there.

    Precautions!
  • This is a different country so don't expect them to know what you are saying.
  • Japan is a nice place where the people are very trusting. please don't ruin tourists image even more.
  • Anime fans, please don't try to speak Japanese leaned from anime, and don't be surprised there are no subtitles in real life.
