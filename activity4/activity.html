Hi, want to find a good buffet to go to? Either you are visiting New York in the Queens area, taking a date to a nice resturant, or you are very hungry and want to get your moneys worth. we got you covered. Finding the nicest and best asian buffets is what we are the best at.

How do we find these buffets?

We find our resturants on our website by finding and visiting every all you can eat buffets there are in Queens, we then select the ones with the best prices compared to the food they serve to add to our website.

Will there be more buffets added?

Definetly, we are constantly looking for all you can eat buffets in Queens and will update our site every time we find a good resturant.
